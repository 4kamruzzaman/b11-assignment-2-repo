Standard Curriculum

  • Kinder (3-6) Years

    Our kindergarten program focuses on holistic development through play-based learning, emphasizing social, emotional, physical, and cognitive growth. We ensure a strong foundation for future education by nurturing curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning in a safe and supportive environment.

    Read More

  • Elementary School

    Our elementary school program builds on the foundation laid in kindergarten, offering a comprehensive curriculum that includes language arts, mathematics, science, social studies, and the arts. We aim to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning in our students, preparing them for future academic success.

    Read More

  • Middle (10-16) Years

    Our middle school program is designed to support students through the critical transition from childhood to adolescence. We offer a balanced curriculum that includes advanced subjects, extracurricular activities, and personal development programs to prepare students for high school and beyond, fostering independence and responsibility.

We look forward to organizing more such enriching and fun-filled activities that contribute to the holistic development of our students.
